We create flow between Business, People and Technology

Tenco is a service design studio based in Malmö, Sweden. We design holistic customer and user experiences that create value for business.

We use service design methodology to understand the crucial intersections between these factors, and design solutions that make work and life easier, clearer and more valuable for everyone.

Case studies

What we do

Supporting business growth

We help organisations to define objectives and justify investment in line with social and market trends. And we support internal process innovation, to create more effective and aligned ways of working.

Harvesting customer insights

We get inside customers’ heads to make sure business goals are aligned with what people really want and need.

Making sense of technology

We help organisations prepare for change by understanding how new technologies can assist and affect customers, users, and businesses as a whole.

Eliminating friction, creating flow

Businesses are made for and by people. Flow for business is created when products and services seamlessly respond to human needs, business goals and broader trends in technology and society.

We use service design methodology to understand the crucial intersections between these factors, and design solutions that make work and life easier, clearer, and more valuable for everyone.

About Us

Tenco has been designing people-centric solutions in Malmö since 2008. We work with clients across the private and public sector, from all industries.

We are a multidisciplinary and international team, with competencies covering user experience,interaction and graphic design, concept development, programming, communications and branding, innovation management, organisational design, and sustainability consulting.

We like big challenges, small details, and gathering around our office piano.
