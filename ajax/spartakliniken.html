Re-thinking the experience of parenting

client Spartakliniken

We helped Spartakliniken understand the changing needs and expectations of modern families, and created a holistic customer experience concept that integrates new and and existing parts of their service.

‘it’s completely changed the way we think about our business’ - Hampus

We took an outside in perspective, analysing Sparta’s business model, operational processes, competitive landscape and the needs of their existing and target clients. Through our research we recognised that a customer’s experience stretches far beyond an appointment, that the needs of every parent and family are different, and there can be no ‘one size fits all’ health care experience.

We developed a concept for an app and a physical hub that gives parents a transparent overview of the services available to them, connects disparate parts of the clinic’s offer including partner and affiliate services, reconfigures client/ client communication, and provides opportunities for parents to further share knowledge and build community. It acknowledges the unique needs of every parent, while also making more effective use of the clinic’s resources and providing an easier and more attractive way for new clients to access the service.
