customer insights to drive change

client Primärvården Skåne

We helped Primärvården Skåne to understand how their clients perceived and accessed information from them, which ultimately informed a re-design of their brand and web platform.

‘We are now at the forefront of e-services in primary care. Our home pages are much more user friendly and we are working in a way that is less restricted to what organisations we belong to internally, which ultimately benefits the users’ - name and job title

Through qualitative research into customer’s behaviour, needs and expectations we discovered that most customers didn’t refer to Primärvården Skåne and their services in the same way as the organisation did internally, and that a lot of information communicated on the website was being overlooked. Through understanding what customers actually needed from the service, we worked to prioritise the functions people used on the site, and made recommendations that helped better align the brand with their business strategy.
