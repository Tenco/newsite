organisational design for a digital transformation

client SKÅNETRAFIKEN

We supported Skånetrafiken with business analysis, benchmark research and ultimately the design of a new internal team, as the organisation geared up for a digital transformation.

We aligned insights that we gathered from different internal and external stakeholders with Skånetrafiken’s future strategic objectives to lay the foundation for a dedicated DevOps organisation with a stronger user experience focus.

This gave Skånetrafiken the capacity to digitise their service and perform governance over their projects, to implement agile work processes, and to work more cross functionally as an organisation rather than in silos .
